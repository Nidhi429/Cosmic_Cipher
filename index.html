COSMIC CIPHER

Decode and Discover

Can You Escape the Unknown?





In a universe filled with endless wonders, you and your fellow astronaut embark on a daring mission aboard the legendary spaceship Galactic Voyager. Your goal is to explore uncharted territories of the cosmos and uncover the mysteries of distant galaxies. But fate has other plans.





As Galactic Voyager soars through the stars, an unexpected anomaly strikes. The ship trembles violently, alarms blare, and before you know it, you crash-land on an uncharted planet in a far-off galaxy. Stranded and with your ship severely damaged, hope seems lost.

Yet, as you survey your surroundings, you discover an ancient spacecraft, seemingly abandoned but intact. Eager to escape, you approach the ship, only to find it locked tight. On the side panel, a cryptic message glimmers under the alien sun:

"Beneath foreign skies where starlight bends,

The key to escape is where your journey ends.

Seek the name of this world unknown,

Unlock the path and find your way home."





Panic sets in. You have no idea where you are or what planet you stand upon. Time is running out. With the atmosphere swirling with unknown dangers, you and your partner make a daring decision: you will split up to explore different regions of the planet. Each area holds unique challenges that you must overcome, and with each challenge you face, you may uncover clues that bring you closer to the chance of escape
